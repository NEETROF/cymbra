# Bundled score credits

Every file below is an **uncompressed MusicXML** transcription of a musical work
that is in the **public domain** (the composer died well over 70 years ago, so
the composition carries no copyright). These short transcriptions were authored
for this project and are released under the repository's licence; they may be
freely redistributed.

If a transcription is ever replaced by one sourced from a third party, record the
source URL and its licence here and drop anything whose licence cannot be
confirmed as public-domain / CC0.

| File | Work | Composer | Status |
|------|------|----------|--------|
| `beginner/ode_to_joy.musicxml` | Ode to Joy (theme from Symphony No. 9) | Ludwig van Beethoven (1770–1827) | Public domain (composition) |
| `beginner/twinkle.musicxml` | Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star | Traditional | Public domain |
| `intermediate/minuet_in_g.musicxml` | Minuet in G major, BWV Anh. 114 | Christian Petzold (1677–1733) | Public domain (composition) |
| `advanced/prelude_e_minor.musicxml` | Prelude in E minor, Op. 28 No. 4 (opening) | Frédéric Chopin (1810–1849) | Public domain (composition) |

All files are short excerpts intended to exercise the notation parser and the
Partition renderer (grand staff, ties, beams, tuplets, chords, accidentals, and
directions), not complete performing editions.
